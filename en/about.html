BRUNA ANTUNES

ABOUT BRUNA
ANTUNES

In 2021, Bruna competed in the FIM Enduro World Championship, where she achieved an impressive 9th place finish overall. This accomplishment highlights her skill and determination on the international stage, further cementing her reputation as a rising star in the enduro community.

Throughout her career, Bruna has contributed significantly to the success of the national team, often being a leading figure in the women’s category at various national and international competitions.

Bruna is actively involved in promoting enduro racing in Portugal. She has participated in numerous national events, including the Campeonato Nacional de Enduro Sprint Motoni 2022, where she continued to showcase her impressive abilities and sportsmanship.
